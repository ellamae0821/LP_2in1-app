    Tutorial for Print Replica - App

How to Log In

• Open the Honolulu Star-Advertiser App and tap on the menu from the upper left and tap on Login.
• Once you are logged in, click on PRINT REPLICA from the menu and it will open the Print Replica page.

How to Log Out

• Click on the Options icon , then select Account tab and click on Logout.

Reading the Article

• Clicking on an area of the page image displayed on the left side will load that content into the right side. If the device is on Portrait mode, page image is displayed on top and the content will be on the bottom.

Accessing Links

• Clicking on the article's page links shows the continuation page of the article as it is on the physical print.
• If the link is for an outside source, such as another website, it will open a new tab for the said link.

Changing Font Size

• When an article is loaded for viewing in the right side of the screen (or bottom side of screen when device is on portrait), pinch in or out to adjust font size.

Changing the Page

• Clicking the arrows on the right and left sides of the page image will change pages.
• You may also tap on the next section arrows to jump to the next or previous section.
• Alternatively, you may swipe right and left sides of the page image to change page.

Zooming my View

• On Browse mode , single tap will open the content in Index viewing mode. Double tap on the page image will zoom in or out.
• On Index viewing mode , double tap will zoom in the area.
• Alternatively, you can pinch in or out on the image page to zoom view.

Search Past Editions

• To access previous issues, click on the Editions icon at the menu panel and select from the recent editions listed.

Print / Download / Share an Article

• To share an article, the share icon will open a Sharing menu which allows you to select from several methods (Email, Print, Facebook, Twitter, etc).

Listen to an Article

• To listen to an article, while on Index viewing mode , click on an article you want to listen.
• On the right side, click Listen to Article icon from the top control area, then a play button will be available for you click and start the audio.

What is the Pages icon for?

• From the Pages Mode you can see small images of all pages in the edition you are viewing. This is an easy way to quickly scan through the entire publication. Click on any page image to open it for viewing. Most publications also allow downloading pages as PDF files from this viewing mode.

Reading an article while offline

• The article needs to be downloaded first before you can read it offline. Click on the options icon , and select Offline mode under Network.
• Additionaly, you may select the number of editions to store on the options below Network.
